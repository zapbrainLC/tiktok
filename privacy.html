Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

This App allows users to authenticate with TikTok to upload their own videos. We collect only the following data:

  • TikTok user ID (provided after login)
  • Videos you select for upload
  • Basic account info required by TikTok's API

We do not sell or share data with third parties. Data is used solely for enabling login and video publishing via the TikTok API.
